Account Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their Dalabapay account and associated personal data at any time.

To request account deletion, please send an email with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

Email: dalabapays@gmail.com

Once received, we will:

  • Verify the account owner
  • Permanently delete the user account
  • Delete personal data including name, email, phone number, and wallet information

Some transaction records may be retained for legal and regulatory purposes for up to 12 months, after which they will be permanently deleted.
